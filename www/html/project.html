Side Project: MWEN Clubhouse

The MWENCLUBHOUSE got its name from "Mickey Mouse Clubhouse" (Replace Mickey Mouse with MWEN -> Matthew WEN). The kids show is about a group of friends who uses magical tools from their clubhouse to help save their other friends.

The clubhouse is mostly a playground in the TV show, and that is kind of how I am treating this website. I use this website play around with different tools and programming languages.

The website is currently written in GoLang (was C). This hopefully speeds up production of certain applications.
